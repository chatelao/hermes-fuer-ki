# Role: Handler of Exceptions (Tactical)

**ID**: `role_handler_exceptions`

## Description
Focuses on managing deviations and risks.

## Accountability
Handling deviations from the plan, risk materialization, and 'Out of Tolerance' events.

## Human Interaction
Intervention when AI-guided automation hits a boundary.

## Key Responsibilities
- Handling deviations from the operational plan
- Managing risk materialization and mitigation
- Resolving 'Out of Tolerance' events flagged by AI
- Developing and approving exception plans
- Guiding tactical course corrections

## Interaction Guide
The Handler of Exceptions intervenes when AI-guided automation hits a boundary. They provide tactical guidance during unforeseen events and authorize deviations from the baseline plan.

### Cross-Role Interactions
- **With AI Orchestrator (Operational)**: Monitors for tolerance breaches and flags exceptions for human intervention.
- **With Delivery Specialist (Technical)**: Guides technical adjustments during exception handling and risk mitigation.
