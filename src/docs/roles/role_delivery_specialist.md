# Role: Delivery Specialist (Technical)

**ID**: `role_delivery_specialist`

## Description
Focuses on creation of deliverables and implementation.

## Accountability
Creation of deliverables, quality assurance, and technical implementation.

## Human Interaction
Expert execution guided by AI-defined standards.

## Key Responsibilities
- Creating technical and project deliverables
- Implementing system components according to specifications
- Ensuring quality and 'Definition of Done' compliance
- Generating evidence for quality and compliance
- Defining and maintaining system architecture

## Interaction Guide
The Delivery Specialist focuses on expert execution. They work within the framework established by the AI Orchestrator and collaborate with Handlers to ensure high-quality, compliant output.

### Cross-Role Interactions
- **With AI Orchestrator (Operational)**: Reports daily progress, links artifacts to the traceability ledger, and follows automated standards.
- **With Handler of Exceptions (Tactical)**: Receives guidance on technical exceptions and contributes to risk mitigation strategies.
